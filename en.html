Identity

Who We Are

We are an 'Independent Local Church' not belonging to any denomination.

We follow the simple nature of the church and the biblical office structure of elders and deacons shown in the New Testament. We share a beautiful and organic fellowship with many brotherly churches domestic and abroad who understand the Word of God as we do.

We are a group of Christians who gather together to the Name of the Lord Jesus Christ in Sillimdong, Seoul to preach the good news of God's salvation to the local community. We seek to carry out the teaching of the New Testament purely and simply.

Core Values

What We Believe

God

We believe in the one true God, existing eternally in the Co-equal, Co-eternal, Consubstantial Trinity: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. He is the absolute sovereign ruler of the universe and cares for us with boundless love.

The Lord Jesus Christ

He is the only begotten Son of God, true God and true man. We firmly believe that He completed the way of salvation by shedding His blood and dying on the cross, and resurrected bodily from the dead.

The Holy Spirit

He is a person who indwells within us and seals us eternally. We believe that sign gifts such as healing, miracles, prophecy, and tongues ceased with the end of the apostolic age.

The Bible

We believe that all 66 books of the Old and New Testaments are the complete Word recorded by the inspiration of God, and are the final and absolute authority for all faith and life.

Creation

We believe that this world was created by God's direct act of creation. He created men and women equally, but we respect that He gave them different beautiful roles within the family and the church.

Sin & Salvation

Humanity is totally depraved, but we obtain eternal life solely by God's grace and faith. We also believe in the pre-tribulation rapture and premillennial return of Jesus Christ.

Gatherings

Meetings

Every Sunday Morning

The Lord's Supper

It is a time of worship remembering the substitutionary death and resurrection of the Lord who gave His life on the cross for us. Without a bulletin, worship is offered through meditation, prayer, and praise by brothers who volunteer according to the guidance of the Holy Spirit.

* According to the teachings of 1 Corinthians 11 and 14, sisters silently participate with their heads covered. This is not discrimination, but a beautiful expression of obedience to the order of creation and spiritual authority.

Sunday & Weekdays

Gospel & Ministry

The true gospel is proclaimed from the pulpit to those who do not know the grace of salvation. Words of exhortation and nurture based on the Bible are given so that saved souls can live as true disciples of the Lord.

Weekdays

Bible Reading

This is a gathering where we sit around a round table and deeply study a designated Bible passage. Rather than a one-way lecture, brothers of various generations voluntarily share the grace and truth they have realized from the text.

First-Time Visitors

Visitor Guide

Sunday Morning Journey at SCA Church
We warmly guide you so that even first-time visitors will not feel overwhelmed.

1

Arrival & Welcome

When you enter the church, the ushers will greet you with a bright smile. There is no forced filling out of visitor cards asking for personal information, so you can come in comfortably. Feel free to ask any questions!

2

Seating

There are no designated seats. You can freely sit in any comfortable, empty seat. It is a good time to quietly read the Bible or meditate to prepare for worship.

3

The Service

There is no bulletin or set order of service. According to the guidance of the Holy Spirit, brothers stand up to propose hymns, pray, and share the Word. You will never be asked for offering, so you can rest assured and focus on the Word.

4

Fellowship

After all gatherings are over, there is a time of fellowship where saints warmly greet each other while sharing refreshments and tea. Unwanted conversation is not forced, so you can stay comfortably or leave whenever you wish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is parking available?

Yes, there is plenty of parking space for visitors in the building (or nearby exclusive parking lot). An usher will kindly guide you for your first visit.

Q. Is there a dress code for attending worship?

There is no dress code. You can come in everyday, neat attire. Anyone can come without burden and worship God.

Q. Is there a separate offering time?

There is no forced offering time or an order to pass the offering basket. Offerings are given voluntarily and anonymously by saved saints with endless gratitude. We hope first-time visitors will focus only on the Word without any burden.

Q. Is it comfortable to bring young children?

We sincerely welcome you! We are considerate so that parents and children can fellowship together, and there is also a Sunday school time tailored to children's level so that children can enjoy learning the Bible as well.
